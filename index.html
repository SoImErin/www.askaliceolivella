WHAT I DO

I offer a wide range of services to make your work and home life a much easier experience

  • Private tutoring; English As an Additional Language (Adults also), Numeracy and Literacy (4-11 years old) Specialising in Dyslexia, ADHD and other additional learning needs.
  • Babysitting and childminding
  • Pet/Housesitting and dog walking
  • Cleaning and garden care
  • Wait at home service; if you are expecting a delivery or service call and can't be at home... I can wait for you

If I can't help you, I will help you find someone who can.

My fees reflect the situation. I take into account my costs (eg travel) and your needs. For tutoring, I can offer special rates for low income families etc Please contact me for more details.
