TUTORING, CHILDCARE AND PETSITTING

MAKING EVERYDAY LIFE EASIER

I offer a variety of services designed to make your home and work life simpler and less stressful.

  • Private Tutoring: English as an Additional Language (including adults), Numeracy and Literacy (ages 4-11). I specialise in supporting learners with Dyslexia, ADHD, and other additional learning needs.
  • Babysitting and Childminding
  • Pet and House Sitting, in your home.
  • Cleaning and Garden Care
  • “Wait at Home” Service: If you're expecting a delivery or service call and can't be there, I can wait on your behalf.

If I'm unable to help personally, I'll do my best to connect you with someone who can.

My rates are flexible and based on your specific needs and circumstances, including factors such as travel costs. For tutoring, I offer special rates for low-income families. Please get in touch for more details.
